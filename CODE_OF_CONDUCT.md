# Code of Conduct

## 1. Statement on Diversity and Inclusivity

We are committed to fostering a welcoming, diverse, and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, or religion. We expect all team members and contributors to treat each other with respect and professionalism.

## 2. Expected and Unacceptable Behaviour

**Expected Behaviour:**

- Be respectful and considerate of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the team and the project.
- Communicate collaboratively and openly.

**Unacceptable Behaviour:**

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission.

## 3. Consequences of Unacceptable Behaviour

Unacceptable behavior from any team member or contributor will not be tolerated. Consequences for violating this code of conduct may include:

- A formal warning from the team.
- Temporary or permanent exclusion from project repositories.

## 4. Reporting and Enforcement Procedure

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please report it by:

1. Reaching out directly to the individual involved to resolve the issue amicably if you feel comfortable doing so.
2. If the issue persists or is severe, notifying the rest of the maintainers immediately.
